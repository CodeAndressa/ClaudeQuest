# Product

## Register

product

## Users

ClaudeQuest is used by internal learners who need guided, practical training in the Claude ecosystem, and by administrators who operate content, users, gamification, certificates, AI settings, and analytics. Users are usually in a task-focused workflow: continue studying, review progress, manage platform data, or prepare the system for broader organizational use.

## Product Purpose

ClaudeQuest is a gamified learning platform for forming professionals in practical use of Claude Chat, Claude Cowork, Claude Code, Prompt Engineering, MCP, Skills, Subagents, and Hooks. Success means learners can progress through real missions with trustworthy XP/progress feedback, while administrators can eventually operate the platform without direct database access.

## Brand Personality

Focused, capable, encouraging. The interface should feel like a serious internal training tool with enough warmth to keep learning approachable.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition inside the authenticated app, fake links to unfinished features, decorative dashboards without real data, and admin screens that imply CRUD capabilities before the backend exists.

## Design Principles

- Make state honest: unavailable workflows should be visibly pending, not silently simulated.
- Keep learning momentum visible through progress, next actions, and clear completion states.
- Prefer operational clarity over spectacle, especially in admin and dashboard surfaces.
- Preserve trust by matching UI affordances to implemented backend capabilities.
- Design for the future SaaS/admin scope without blocking the current MVP.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA, keyboard navigation, readable contrast in dark and light themes, translated UI in pt-BR/en-US/es-ES, and reduced-motion-safe interactions.
